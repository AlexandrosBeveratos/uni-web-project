OUR PHILOSOPHY

Our team is client-oriented and we work our way to win your trust. We take trust of all clients seriously, so we think reliability of our fleet and its constant improvement is very important. We also think there needs to be an affordable option for people to choose so we try to have the best prices.

OUR VISION

We look forward into what is coming for us in the future and eventually what new our clients will experience. The team's hard work will pay out and it will grow even more while trying to satisfy more customers.

OUR GOAL

The mutual goal we have is constant self-improvement while also expanding our work. We want to open more rental shops in Europe and collaborate with other car makers aswell to have the ability to deliver the best vehicles in their best state

HUMAN RESOURCES

CALL CENTER DEPARTMENT

The Call Center plays an important role in our company as it is in charge of order calls. They also answer about all the Information needed by the customers, as well as receive any complaints or notices.

The Rental Department is in charge of all the rentals of the past, current and future. They also make sure the vehicles are good for use before the next rental.

The Transport Department is in charge of all the VIP transportation of the customers as well as of the quality of its vehicles.

LOGISTICS DEPARTMENT

The Logistics Department is taking care of all the logistical problems as well as the pricing of the services. Their team is also taking care of fleet's expenses.
